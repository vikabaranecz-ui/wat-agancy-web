# WAT? Agency – Lead Research Assistant

## Project Overview

WAT? Agency is a marketing bureau based in Antwerp that builds complete marketing systems for renovation and insulation companies. Services include Google Business optimization, websites, social media management, and client testimonial systems.

**Target market:** Renovation and insulation contractors in and around Antwerp, Belgium.

## Lead Research Assistant

This assistant helps WAT? Agency identify, research, and qualify potential clients — renovation and insulation companies that would benefit from a done-for-you marketing system.

### Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

- **Industry:** Renovatie, isolatie, dakwerken, schrijnwerk, schilderwerk, vloerleggers
- **Location:** Antwerpen, Gent, Mechelen, Leuven, Brussels region
- **Size:** 1–25 employees (independent contractors, small teams)
- **Pain points:**
  - No online presence or outdated website
  - No Google Business profile or low rating/reviews
  - No consistent social media content
  - Rely entirely on word-of-mouth
  - Too busy with jobs to do their own marketing
- **Buying signals:**
  - Recently started business (< 3 years)
  - Active on job platforms (Werkspot, Batibouw leads)
  - Posting on Facebook groups for tradespeople
  - Google profile with few or no reviews

### Disqualifiers

- Already works with a marketing agency
- National franchise or large company (> 50 employees)
- Outside the target geography
- No online footprint at all (impossible to research)

- `/research-lead` — Deep-dive research on a specific company
