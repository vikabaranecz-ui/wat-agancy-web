# WAT? Agency — Marketing Context

*Single source of truth for brand positioning, voice, and audience. Reference this before any outreach or content task.*

---

## Product Overview

WAT? Agency builds done-for-you marketing systems for renovation and insulation contractors. The core offering is a complete, managed marketing stack — not a single service, but the whole system: Google Business, website, social media, and review/testimonial management.

**Core promise:** "Meer klanten, zonder dat jij er tijd in steekt."

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Owner-operators of small renovation and insulation businesses (1–10 employees) in the Antwerp region who are great at their trade but not at marketing.

**Secondary:** Small teams (10–25 employees) who have grown beyond word-of-mouth but haven't invested in a proper marketing system yet.

**Geography:** Antwerpen, Gent, Mechelen, Leuven, Brussels (Vlaams/Brussels).

**Industries:** Renovatie, isolatie, dakwerken, schrijnwerk, schilderwerk, vloerleggers, algemene aanneming.

---

## Personas

### "De Vakman" (primary)
- Age: 30–50
- Runs business alone or with 2–3 workers
- Gets all work via word-of-mouth and existing clients
- Knows he needs a website but hasn't found time
- Skeptical of marketing agencies ("te duur", "werkt toch niet")
- Responds to peer proof and specific, local examples

### "De Groeier" (secondary)
- Age: 35–55
- Team of 5–20, doing well but wants more volume
- Has a basic website but no consistent marketing
- Has tried Facebook ads or a freelancer without results
- Wants a professional partner, not a freelancer

## Pain Points (customer language)

- "Ik heb geen tijd om dat allemaal bij te houden"
- "Mijn website is oud maar ik weet niet waar te beginnen"
- "Klanten vinden me niet op Google"
- "Ik heb bijna geen reviews"
- "Ik doe alles via via, maar het wordt onregelmatig"
- "Ik heb al eens iets geprobeerd maar het leverde niks op"

---

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct competitors:** Generalist marketing agencies in Antwerp (often too expensive and not specialized), freelance web designers (no ongoing support), DIY platforms (Wix, Squarespace — no strategy).

**Indirect competitors:** Doing nothing (biggest competitor), Werkspot / lead platforms (transactional, no brand building).

**WAT? Agency differentiators:**
- Niche: only renovation and insulation — deep knowledge of the industry
- Done-for-you: not coaching, not tools — we execute
- Local: Antwerp-based, knows the market
- Results-focused: positioned around more clients, not more likes

---

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Direct, no-nonsense, warm. Like a trusted local expert, not a corporate agency.

**Do:** Short sentences. Concrete claims. Specific examples. Belgian Dutch.
**Don't:** Jargon, buzzwords ("synergy", "360° approach"), overpromising.

**Register:** "je/jij" in outreach unless signals suggest otherwise. Never "u" in cold outreach.

**Language:** Dutch (Flemish). No formal or Dutch Netherlands Dutch — write as Antwerpers talk.

---

## Proof Points

- Renovation and insulation specialist positioning (stand out vs. generalist agencies)
- Done-for-you system (owners don't need to learn or manage)
- Google Business optimization as lead driver (measurable, local)
- Testimonial/review system builds long-term trust signal

## Content & SEO Context

**Primary keywords (Dutch):** renovatie Antwerpen, isolatie Antwerpen, dakwerken Antwerpen, [service] [stad]

**Content themes for leads' social feeds:**
- Before/after project photos
- Short video walkthroughs of jobs
- Customer testimonials (video preferred)
- Seasonal tips (isolatie voor de winter, dakcontrole)
- Behind-the-scenes (team at work)

## Goals (WAT? Agency business)

1. Sign 2–3 new renovation/insulation clients per month
2. Build case studies from early clients for social proof
3. Establish as the go-to marketing partner for the trade sector in Antwerp
